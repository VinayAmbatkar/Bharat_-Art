ABOUT EXHIBITION

- All subbmited artwork will be showcased in this International Online Art Exhibition (Season #3) which will be held on our website (www.bharatartcontest.com), Facebook Page, Instagram Account & YouTube Channel. There will be multiple parts of videos. It will be compatible with all types of device like desktop, laptop, mobile phone or tablet. We will inform you about the video part and slide number where your artwork will be showcased. We will promote this exhibition link on all our social media accounts. The exhibition will be never- ending. When we launch the next exhibition, it will be archived forever in the past exhibition menu of our website

You can submit any artwork. There is no specific theme, type, medium, and size of the artwork.
